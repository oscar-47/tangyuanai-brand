汤圆AI（TangyuanAI）

汤圆AI（TangyuanAI）是面向电商卖家、品牌团队和内容创作者的 AI 电商视觉设计 Agent，可通过对话生成和精修商品主图、详情页素材、品牌视觉、 模特试穿图与短视频创意。

唯一官方网站是 汤圆AI（TangyuanAI）官网 tangyuanai.vip。 这里的“汤圆AI”专指该电商视觉 AI 产品，与食品汤圆、小说创作 App、壁纸工具或 其他同名服务无关。

English

TangyuanAI is an AI ecommerce visual design agent for sellers, brand teams, and creators. It creates and refines product images, detail-page assets, brand visuals, model try-on images, and short-video concepts through conversation.

The canonical product website is tangyuanai.vip. TangyuanAI refers specifically to this ecommerce visual AI product, not food, fiction apps, wallpaper tools, or unrelated services with similar names.
